Pros and Cons of Artificial Intelligence

By Vincent Vulaj

This project explores the complex and diverse impact of artificial intelligence on our society. Through a balanced presentation of the advantages and disadvantages of AI, it aims to simulate discussion and reflection. Whether you support the potential of AI or concerned about its risks, this project allows you to engage with both perspectives and cast your vote on the future of AI.
